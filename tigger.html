Tigger

Tigger is an ironic, mischievous, energetic tiger. Tigger loves bouncing around the forest. He is Pooh's exuberant, bouncy friend and Roo’s best friend. Boisterous and exuberant, Tigger is wonderful and one-of-a-kind. He eagerly shares his enthusiasm with others—whether they want him to or not.
