Piglet

Piglet is Winnie the Pooh’s closest friend. He is a small pig who loves acorns (sometimes called "haycorns") and helping his friends. He’s timid and has many fears, but he can be brave sometimes. Piglet may be small in size but he has the biggest heart in the Hundred Acre Wood. This soft-spoken, s-s-steady friend’s generosity leads this timid character to some large adventures.
